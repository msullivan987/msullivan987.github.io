How to make a sandwich

  1. Liberally spread one slice of bread with crunchy peanut butter
  2. Completely cover a second slice of bread with your choice of jam or fruit preserves
  3. Place the two slices of bread together, spread sides facing each other
  4. Cut the sandwich in triangles. If you accidentally cut the sandwich into rectangles, start again at step 1.
